Doggos for Days

Who let the dogs out? This is a page dedicated to all the puppers and good boys. Every dog has its day—and that's basically every day if you're a dog owner!

These Ankle-Biters are Top Dog

Dachshunds, aka Wiener Dogs or Doxies, are very loyal, hyper vigilant, and vertically-challenged. Hot digitty dog! 10 out of 10

The name “Shih Tzu” means little lion. But I Shih Tzu not, there is nothing fierce about this breed. These sweet pooches are lovers, not hunters. 10 out of 10

Maltese puppies are fairly easy to train, given their smaller size and their quick minds. This breed is a diamond in the ruff! 10 out of 10

Pomeranians aren't just popular because they're so paw-sitively cute. They have extroverted, smart personalities that make them extra lovable. 10 out of 10
