Some of Our Favorite Small Breeds

Dachshunds, aka Wiener Dogs or Doxies, are loyal, hyper vigilant, and vertically-challenged. Hot digitty dog! 10 out of 10

“Shih Tzu” means little lion, but there's nothing fierce about this breed. These sweet pooches are lovers, not hunters. 10 out of 10

Maltese puppies are fairly easy to train, given their smaller size and their quick minds. This breed is a diamond in the ruff! 10 out of 10

Pomeranians aren't just popular because they're so cute. They are also loved for their extroverted, smart personalities. 10 out of 10
