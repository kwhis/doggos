Little Dog, Big Bark

Dachshunds, aka Wiener Dogs or Doxies, are very loyal, hyper vigilant, and vertically-challenged. Hot digitty dog! 10 out of 10

The name “Shih Tzu” means little lion. But I Shih Tzu not, there is nothing fierce about this breed. These sweet pooches are lovers, not hunters. 10 out of 10

Maltese puppies are fairly easy to train, given their smaller size and their quick minds. This breed is a diamond in the ruff! 10 out of 10

Pomeranians aren't just popular because they're so paw-sitively cute! They have smart, extroverted personalities that make them extra lovable. 10 out of 10

I Like Big Mutts and I Cannot Lie

The Saint Bernard can weigh up to 180 pounds. They are most famous for rescuing humans from Swiss Alps snowstorms. Give them a round of ap-paws! 10 out of 10

Don't go barking up the wrong tree! German Shepherds can be total softies, despire their intimidating looks. They are considered to be family-dogs, but be aware they can be suspicious of strangers. 10 out of 10

This dog isn't big, it is just a little husky! Siberian Huskies have thick, double-fur coats and striking eye colors. They are recognized by distinctive markings and a curled tail. 10 out of 10

Last but not leashed, is the Boxer. Boxers have bubbly and adventurous personailities. This breed is very protective of their family and they love children. 10 out of 10
